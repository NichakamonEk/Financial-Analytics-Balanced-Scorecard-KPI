## Project Overview
This project evaluates the business performance of **Jubilee Superstores** through two integrated components:

1. **Financial performance analysis** using interactive dashboards  
2. **Strategic Balanced Scorecard (BSC) and KPI development** aligned with long-term business and ESG objectives  

The project goes beyond reporting by translating financial and non-financial insights into a **structured performance management framework** that supports sustainable value creation.

---

## Business Context
Jubilee Superstores is a multi-category retailer operating across products, regions, and customer segments.  
Management requires visibility not only into profitability, but also into the **drivers of performance, sustainability risks, and ethical responsibilities**.

Key challenges identified include:
- Strong operational efficiency but weak gross margin performance  
- Variations in profitability across regions, divisions, and channels  
- Increasing expectations around ESG, reconciliation, and ethical data governance
